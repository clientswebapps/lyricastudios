Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026 | Company: LyricaStudios

At LyricaStudios, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order or interact with our platform, we may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Contact details (if provided)
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party payment providers)
  • Questionnaire responses (stories, names, themes, messages for your custom song)
  • Any additional information you voluntarily provide

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and deliver your personalized AI-generated song
  • Process orders and payments
  • Communicate with you regarding your order, revisions, or support
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send important updates related to your order

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Sharing

We may share limited information only with trusted third parties when necessary, such as:

  • Payment processors (for secure transactions)
  • Service providers that help us operate our platform (e.g., hosting, email delivery)

These third parties are required to protect your data and use it only for service-related purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. However, no online system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Song Content and Inputs

  • The details you provide (stories, names, messages) are used solely to create your custom song.
  • Sensitive or personal content is handled with confidentiality and used only for production purposes.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Complete your order
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal or accounting requirements

After this period, your data may be securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data usage (where applicable)

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies or similar technologies to improve website performance and understand user behavior. You can adjust your browser settings to disable cookies if you prefer.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13 (or applicable local age of consent). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, you may contact us at:
